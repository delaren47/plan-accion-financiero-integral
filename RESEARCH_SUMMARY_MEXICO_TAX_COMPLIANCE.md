## Research Coverage (20 Sections)

### Core Tax Requirements

#### 1. RFC (Registro Federal de Contribuyentes)
- 13-character tax ID (equivalent to EIN)
- **Cost:** $0 (free registration)
- **Deadline:** Within first month of operations
- **Process:** Online or physical SAT office
- **Documents:** CURP, birth certificate, proof of address, ID
- **Official link:** https://www.sat.gob.mx/tramites/operacion/28753/obten-tu-rfc-con-la-clave-unica-de-registro-de-poblacion-curp

#### 2. e.firma / FIEL (Digital Signature)
- Electronic signature required for all SAT transactions
- **Validity:** 4 years (must renew)
- **Cost:** $0 (free)
- **Process:** Physical appointment at SAT office (mandatory)
- **Appointment link:** https://citas.sat.gob.mx/
- **Critical:** Cannot file taxes or issue invoices without it

#### 3. ISR (Corporate Income Tax)
- **Rate:** 30% flat for corporations (Personas Morales)
- **Progressive:** 1.92%-35% for individuals (Personas Físicas)
- **Fiscal year:** Calendar year (Jan 1 - Dec 31)
- **Annual filing:** March 31 (corporations), April 30 (individuals)
- **Monthly provisional payments:** Due by **17th of following month**

#### 4. IVA (Value Added Tax - VAT)
- **Rate:** 16% (general), 0% (exports)
- **Monthly filing:** Due by **17th of following month**
- **IVA acreditable:** Can recover IVA paid on business expenses
- **Services to foreigners:** 0% if service used abroad (requires documentation)
- **Retention:** Clients retain 2/3 of IVA (10.67%) when paying service providers

#### 5. CFDI 4.0 (Electronic Invoicing)
- **Mandatory** digital invoices (XML format)
- **PAC providers required:** Authorized certification providers
- **Timing:** Issue within 72 hours of receiving payment
- **Types:** Ingreso (income), Egreso (credit note), Nómina (payroll), Retenciones (withholdings), Pago (payment complement)
- **PAC costs:** $50-$200 USD/month for unlimited invoicing
- **Recommended PACs:** Facturama, Bind ERP, Aspel Facture
- **Critical:** Without valid CFDI, expense is NOT tax deductible

#### 6. DIOT (Monthly Supplier Report)
- Monthly report of ALL purchases from suppliers
- **Deadline:** 17th of following month
- **Purpose:** SAT cross-references your purchases with supplier's sales
- **Penalty:** $1,410-$35,250 pesos for non-filing

#### 7. Contabilidad Electrónica (Electronic Accounting)
- **Catálogo de Cuentas:** Chart of accounts (XML format)
- **Balanza de Comprobación:** Monthly trial balance (XML format)
- **Deadline:** 25th of following month
- **Software required:** ContPAQi, Aspel NOI, Bind ERP ($100-$300 USD/month)
- **Critical:** Highly complex, requires certified accountant

#### 8. REPSE (Service Providers Registry)
- **Required for:** Specialized services to other businesses (consulting, software dev, marketing)
- **Validity:** 3 years (must renew)
- **Cost:** $0 (free registration via STPS)
- **Process:** https://repse.stps.gob.mx/
- **Requirements:** Tax compliance opinion (SAT), IMSS compliance opinion
- **Penalty:** $27,000-$540,000 pesos for operating without REPSE
- **Client impact:** Clients cannot deduct your invoices without your REPSE folio

### Employment & Payroll (If hiring employees)

#### 9. State & Municipal Obligations
- **Payroll tax (Impuesto Sobre Nómina):** 2-3% of total payroll (varies by state)
- **Municipal business license:** Only if physical office (not required for 100% remote)

#### 10. IMSS, INFONAVIT, SAR (Social Security)
- **IMSS registration:** Within 5 days of hiring employee
- **Employer contributions:** 25-30% of employee salary
  - IMSS (health/disability): ~20.4%
  - Riesgos de Trabajo: 0.5-15%
  - INFONAVIT (housing): 5%
  - SAR (retirement): 2%
- **Employee contributions:** 2-3% (withheld from salary)
- **Payment:** Bimonthly (every 2 months), due 17th of following month
- **CFDI Nómina:** Mandatory electronic payroll invoices
- **ISR withholding:** Progressive 1.92-35% on salaries
- **Penalty:** $216-$37,800 pesos PER EMPLOYEE for non-registration (SEVERE)

### Business Formation

#### 11. Business Registration
- **Persona Física con Actividad Empresarial (PFAE):** Sole proprietorship
  - Cost: $0
  - Unlimited liability
  - Simple registration

- **SA de CV (Sociedad Anónima):** Corporation
  - Cost: $1,500-$3,000 USD (notary fees)
  - Limited liability
  - Minimum 2 shareholders
  - Capital requirement: $50,000 pesos

- **S de RL (Sociedad de Responsabilidad Limitada):** LLC equivalent
  - Cost: $1,500-$3,000 USD (notary fees)
  - Limited liability
  - 2-50 partners
  - No minimum capital

- **Notary requirement:** MANDATORY for corporations (cannot incorporate without notary)

### Compliance Costs & Penalties

#### 12. Annual Compliance Costs (2026 Estimates)

**Persona Física con Actividad Empresarial:**
- Contador (accountant): $4,800-$9,600 USD/year
- PAC (invoicing provider): $600-$2,400 USD/year
- Software contable: $600-$1,800 USD/year
- REPSE: $100-$300 USD (one-time/renewal)
- **TOTAL: $6,100-$14,100 USD/year**

**Sociedad Anónima (SA de CV):**
- Constitution (notary): $1,500-$3,000 USD (one-time)
- Contador: $6,000-$12,000 USD/year
- PAC: $600-$2,400 USD/year
- Software: $600-$1,800 USD/year
- Auditoría anual (if revenue >$100M MXN): $2,000-$5,000 USD
- **TOTAL Year 1: $10,000-$25,000 USD**
- **TOTAL Subsequent Years: $9,500-$22,000 USD/year**

**Comparison with Wyoming LLC:**

- Mexico SA de CV: $9,500-$22,000/year (10-22x MORE expensive)

### Special Considerations

#### 14. Foreign Entity Considerations

**Permanent Establishment (Establecimiento Permanente):**
- Triggered if Wyoming LLC has:
  - Physical office in Mexico
  - Employees in Mexico
  - Continuous services in Mexico (>183 days/year)
- **Consequence:** Must pay 30% ISR in Mexico on attributable profits
- **Solution:** Operate as Persona Física Mexicana (you), not as LLC Wyoming

**Transfer Pricing (Precios de Transferencia):**
- Required if related-party transactions and revenue >$13M MXN/year (~$700k USD)
- Study cost: $3,000-$10,000 USD

**Withholding on Foreign Payments:**
- Services to Mexican client from Wyoming LLC: 25% ISR withholding
- Royalties: 25% withholding
- Interest: 10% withholding
- **Solution:** Invoice as Persona Física Mexicana to avoid withholding

**Tax Treaty (Mexico-USA):**
- Can reduce withholding rates
- Requires Form 6166 (US residency certificate)
- Complex application process

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Not hiring accountant from day 1**
   - Consequence: Fines, incorrect filings, lost deductions
   - Solution: Hire certified accountant BEFORE issuing first invoice

2. **Not understanding IVA on invoices**
   - Consequence: Charging incorrect IVA, SAT issues
   - Solution: Confirm with accountant if service is 16%, 0%, or exempt

3. **Not validating received CFDIs**
   - Consequence: Deducting expenses with cancelled invoices (SAT rejects)
   - Solution: Validate UUID on SAT portal before recording expense

4. **Not registering employees in IMSS**
   - Consequence: $216-$37,800 pesos fine PER EMPLOYEE + labor lawsuit
   - Solution: Register within 5 days of hire, or contract as freelancers (honorarios)

5. **Not renewing e.firma on time**
   - Consequence: Cannot file taxes or issue invoices (business paralysis)
   - Solution: Set calendar reminder 60 days before expiration (every 4 years)

6. **Operating without REPSE**
   - Consequence: $27k-$540k pesos fine + clients cannot deduct your invoices
   - Solution: Register REPSE BEFORE providing first service to companies

7. **Paying taxes late (after day 17)**
   - Consequence: Recargos 1.47% monthly + inflation adjustment
   - Solution: Calendar reminder on day 15 each month (2 days before deadline)

8. **Not preserving XML files of CFDIs**
   - Consequence: In SAT audit, without XML cannot prove expense
   - Solution: Automatic backup of XMLs in 2 locations (PAC + your cloud)

9. **Mixing personal and business finances**
   - Consequence: Lose deductions, SAT may reject expenses
   - Solution: Separate business bank account 100% for business

10. **Not filing electronic accounting (day 25)**
    - Consequence: $1,410-$35,250 pesos fine
    - Solution: Accountant should have automated process to submit by day 20-25
